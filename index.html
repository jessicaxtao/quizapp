The following questions appeared in a study done on general knowledge norms among American college students, specifically comparing the results from 1980 and 2012. While the original 1980 quiz had 300 questions, one had to be dropped as it no longer has a correct answer ("What is the capital of Czechoslovakia?").

In the study, students were given half of the questions (150) but here all of them will be presented. Furthermore, spellings that appeared close enough to the researcher was marked as correct but here spelling matters. I tried to include some common mispellings but for the most part if an answer is misspelled it will not be counted as correct. All answers should be a single word. No capitalization is needed. There is no time limit.
